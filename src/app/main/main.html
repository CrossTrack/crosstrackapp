Activ8 Fitness

Activ8 Fitness is a Fitness Tracking App that will allow you to log all of your workouts and keep them organized for you. Activ8 keeps track of all of your Personal Records for when you hit that big lift and shows you your progress for each movement. Activ8 is easy to use and has all your favorite named workouts pre-programmed so when you go out and kick Fran's ass in a time of 2 minutes flat you can select Fran from the dropdown and it pre-populates for you.

Activ8 is a great way to stay active and to really stay on course to reaching your personal fitness goals.
